# Privacy Policy

**BULK Checkbox Checker** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

- The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not send any information to external servers.
- No user data, browsing history, or personal information is collected or shared.
- Any settings or preferences are stored locally in your browser and are not accessible by the developer or third parties.

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
